Frequently Asked Questions

What is ZEROSINT?

ZEROSINT is an advanced open-source intelligence platform that provides various tools for information gathering and analysis.

What tools are available?

We offer multiple tools including WHOIS lookup, DNS lookup, IP analysis, scam checking, Discord ID lookup, email search, username checking, and more.

How accurate are the results?

Our tools gather information from reliable sources and databases, providing accurate and up-to-date results. However, the accuracy may vary depending on the availability and freshness of data sources.

Is it legal to use ZEROSINT?

ZEROSINT is designed for legitimate information gathering purposes. Users must comply with applicable laws and regulations when using our tools.

How do I use the platform?

Simply select the desired tool from the dropdown menu on the homepage, enter your search query, and click the search button to get results.
